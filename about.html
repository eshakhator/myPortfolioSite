About

Who is ether? What is spayce?

Hi there! You may know me as Esha or ether or etherspayce, and I am a CS major who happens to draw from time to time. This is my "spayce", a purposeful misspelling of the word space. I have low vision but I have always had creative hobbies and I didn't let poor eyesight and terrible hand-eye coordination stop me. Digital art allows me to create art more easily and with less strain thanks to features like zoom-in and the stabilizer.

Please check out more of my work at my Instagram and if you're interested, you can buy my work on mugs, totes, stickers, and more on my Redbubble shop!

Take care!
